## Vision

Chimera is building the infrastructure for **unprecedentedly private, secure, and decentralized AI**. 

We're creating an AI fabric that combines:
- 🔐 **Fully Homomorphic Encryption (FHE)** — compute on encrypted data
- 🛡️ **Zero-Knowledge Proofs (ZKP)** — verify without revealing
- 🌐 **Radical Decentralization** — true P2P networks, no central authority
- 🧠 **Self-Evolving AI** — AI that optimizes its own training

> "Beyond government capability in privacy, security, and AI"

## Key Innovations

### AMDL — AI Model Definition Language
A declarative language for defining AI models that compile to training pipelines.

### DTO — Dynamic Task Orchestrator
AI-powered orchestration that optimizes distributed training in real-time.

### Privacy-First by Design
Not an afterthought. FHE, ZKPs, and Differential Privacy built into every layer.
